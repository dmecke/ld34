F-L-O-I-D - Ludum Dare 34

Fork me on GitHub

You are the blue cell in the middle of the screen. Clicking next to it will emit some mass - and make your cell move in the opposite direction. Try to move over other cells to incorporate their mass and grow.

Made with ♥ for Ludum Dare 34
I've used some great free tools and resources to create this game and during the Ludum Dare:
ScreenToGif, Otomata - Generative Musical Sequencer, Audio Cutter online, Google Fonts
