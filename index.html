F-L-O-I-D - Ludum Dare 34

Fork me on GitHub

Hint: If you are stuck, click "back to menu" to retry a level!

This game has been made in 48 hours during the Ludum Dare 34. You are the blue cell in the middle of the screen. Clicking next to it will emit some mass - and make your cell move in the opposite direction. Try to move over other cells to absorb their mass and grow. Heavily inspired by the fantastic game Osmos HD.

Made with ♥ in Hamburg, Germany
I've used some great free tools and resources to create this game:
ScreenToGif, Otomata - Generative Musical Sequencer, Audio Cutter online, Bfxr, Google Fonts
